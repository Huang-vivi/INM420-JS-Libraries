Wilderness Camping

Experience the true essence of the Rockies under a canopy of stars. Pitch your tent in pristine wilderness.

Camping

Disconnect and reconnect with nature. Firewood and bear-proof storage provided at designated sites.

RV Adventures

From $50/night Multi-day

Hit the road with all the comforts of home. Spacious RV sites with full hookups surrounded by epic mountain views.

RV Camping

Enjoy powered sites with easy access to main highways and trailheads. Perfect for family road trips.

Winter · Land

Glacier Exploration

From $110 3–5 hrs

Step onto the ancient Athabasca Glacier. Walk on ice thousands of years old and drink fresh glacial water.

Glacier Trek

Guided Icewalks equipped with crampons. Learn about glaciology and the changing climate firsthand.

Summer · Water

Canoeing

$75/hr 1–3 hrs

Glide across the impossibly turquoise waters of Lake Louise or Moraine Lake with Victoria Glacier as your backdrop.

Canoeing

Canoes for 2–4 people. No experience needed. Life jackets provided. Best in morning for calm water.

Wildlife Watching

Wildlife Safari

Small group tours at dawn or dusk for optimal viewing. Binoculars and spotting scopes provided.
